# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or level of expertise.

## Expected behavior

Be constructive, assume good faith, respect boundaries, accept responsibility for mistakes, and focus criticism on ideas and work rather than people. Handle security-sensitive information with particular care.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sustained disruption, unwanted sexual attention, deliberate exposure of private information, and publication of credentials, forensic evidence, or other sensitive data are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior violates this code. Report conduct concerns privately through an available GitHub contact channel. Do not publish sensitive details in a public issue.

Maintainers will review reports as promptly and impartially as practical and will protect the privacy and safety of reporters and affected people.
